Educational Youtube channels in India

India is home to many influential educational YouTube channels that cater to a wide range of subjects and audiences. Here are a few notable ones:

Dhruv Rathee

Dhruv Rathee is a popular Indian YouTuber, social media influencer, and activist known for his informative and analytical videos on various topics, including politics, environment, and social issues. He gained prominence for his fact-based content and critical analysis of current events, often challenging mainstream narratives. Dhruv's videos aim to educate and raise awareness among his audience, making complex topics more accessible and engaging.

Soch by Mohak mangal

"Soch by Mohak Mangal" is a popular YouTube channel run by Mohak Mangal, an Indian YouTuber known for creating informative and analytical videos on various topics, including social, political, and economic issues. His content aims to educate and raise awareness among viewers by presenting well-researched and evidence-based information. Mohak Mangal's videos often delve into complex subjects, making them accessible and engaging for a wide audience.

Hasi TV

Hasi TV, run by Haseeb Shafaqat, is a popular YouTube channel that focuses on educational content, particularly in the areas of science, technology, and Islamic history. The channel aims to provide viewers with interesting and informative videos on a wide range of topics, including historical events, scientific discoveries, and cultural insights. Haseeb Shafaqat's engaging presentation style and well-researched content have garnered a significant following.
