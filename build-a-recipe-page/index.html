Hyderabadi Biryani

World's famous food from city of pearls "Hyderabad".

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It's made by layering and cooking rice with marinated meat (such as chicken, mutton, or fish), spices, and herbs. Biryani is known for its rich taste, fragrant aroma, and vibrant colors, often garnished with fried onions, boiled eggs, and fresh herbs like cilantro and mint. There are many regional variations of biryani, each with its unique ingredients and preparation methods. It's a beloved dish enjoyed by many around the world.
here is the recipe below...

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken/Mutton/Beef
  • Curd Chutney
  • Onion Rings

Instructions

  1. Mix spices with raw meat
  2. Set it in the bottom of the utensil
  3. Add rice on the top of the akhni(mixture paste of spices)
  4. Cook it on low flame about 15-20 minuttes until the hunger raiser smell comes out
  5. Serve it on plates and eat it with bare hands to have best experience
  6. All set you are good to goooo....
